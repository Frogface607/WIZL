WIZL MYTH BUSTER: "Indica = Sleepy, Sativa = Energetic"

Hot take: the indica/sativa classification is mostly about plant shape, not your high.

THE TRUTH:
- Indica/sativa describes how the PLANT grows (short & bushy vs tall & lanky)
- Your actual experience depends on TERPENES + CANNABINOIDS, not plant shape
- A "sativa" with lots of myrcene can absolutely couch-lock you
- An "indica" with limonene can energize you

WHAT ACTUALLY MATTERS:
1. Terpene profile (the real predictor)
2. THC/CBD ratio
3. Your personal biochemistry
4. Consumption method

Stop choosing by label. Start choosing by profile.

Before breeders started crossing everything with everything, there were landraces — pure, wild cannabis strains that evolved naturally over centuries.

Why they matter: EVERY modern strain traces back to these originals. When you smoke Gelato, you're smoking the great-great-grandchild of Afghani and Thai landraces.

Landraces are the classical music of cannabis. Everything you like samples from them.
