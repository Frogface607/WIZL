# WIZL Poster Prompts — "Untold Stories" Series

A4 portrait posters for printing and dropping off at shops. Each poster is a **still from the middle of an untold story** — a moment from WIZL's adventure built around a famous strain name. Movie/book-cover composition. Strain name as the title.

**Aesthetic:** Mystic Street Futurism — Miyazaki warmth × Moomin linework × Adventure Time playfulness × street-culture neon. See `design/system.md`.

**NO cannabis imagery.** Characters, places, foods, weather, creatures — never leaves or buds. Titles carry the strain reference.

---

## Character (reuse across all posters)

> Kind anthropomorphic weasel in an oversized purple traveling cloak with moon-and-star patches, weathered pointed wizard hat, small soft brown fur. Carries a gnarled wooden staff topped with a glowing emerald crystal (#99F788). Leather satchel holding mysterious vials and a sleeping orange tabby cat peeking out. A worn magical book under his arm. Warm expressive eyes, gentle smile. Small stature against large magical environments.

## Style block (reuse across all posters)

> Hand-drawn illustrated movie poster. Miyazaki atmospheric warmth, Moomin gentle linework, Adventure Time playful simplicity. Deep navy twilight palette (#0B1218) lit by neon-green (#99F788), wizard-purple (#8C6FB8), warm gold (#CE8E58), soft pink (#D48AB5). Bioluminescent plants, drifting magical smoke, amber streetlamps, layered architecture mixing fantasy and retro-futuristic signage. Cozy proportions, rounded shapes, cinematic depth. No text inside the illustration area — leave clean space in the bottom third for the title.

## Title treatment (add in post or via text-capable generator)

- **Top or bottom third:** strain name in heavy condensed sans-serif (Montserrat Black 900), wide tracking, cream `#F2E8D4`.
- **Underneath:** *"A WIZL untold story"* — light 300 weight, letter-spacing 0.3em, muted cream.
- **Bottom margin:** tiny `wizl.space` · `with love` in muted pink `#D48AB5`.

## Output settings

- Aspect ratio: **5:7** (closest to A4) — or 2:3 / 3:4 and crop to A4 in post
- Resolution: 2048px shorter side minimum for print
- Dark background, high contrast, cinematic lighting

## How to use

1. **Generator:** Midjourney (`--ar 5:7 --style raw`), Freepik Spaces, or Leonardo.
2. **Iterate:** generate 3-4 variations per prompt, pick the strongest composition.
3. **Add title in post:** Figma / Photoshop / Canva
   - Strain name: Montserrat Black 900, tracking 0.1em, cream `#F2E8D4`
   - Subtitle: *"A WIZL untold story"* — Montserrat Light 300, tracking 0.3em, muted cream
   - Bottom-right corner: `wizl.space` · `with love` — muted pink `#D48AB5`, tiny
4. **Print:** A4 300dpi, matte paper. Roll up with masking tape — drop off at shops during walks.
5. **QR code** (optional): small bottom-left, links to `wizl.space/strains/{slug}` for that specific strain.

## Series identity

Each poster is numbered (`№ 01`, `№ 02`...) like a print series. Over time builds a collection — some shops will start displaying multiples. Rare posters become collectibles (hand-signed "SK" initial in corner).
